Short Biography

Muhammad Iqbal, later known in life as Allama Sir Muhammad Iqbal, is one of the most prominent figures in the history of Subcontinent. He was born in Sialkot in 1877. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest poets in the Urdu language, but he is most famous for presenting the idea of a free state to the Muslims of the Subcontinent, which was later known as "Pakistan". He died on 21st April, 1938.
